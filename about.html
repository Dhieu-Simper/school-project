About Godhead Elementary School

Nurturing minds, building character, and growing leaders.

Our History

Godhead Elementary School was founded in 2023 by Dhieu Thuch and his wife, Lucy Adior. With a vision to provide holistic education rooted in Christian values, the school emphasizes technology, leadership, and entrepreneurship for the young generation of South Sudan.

Mission

To nurture pupils academically, socially, and spiritually so they become responsible and compassionate leaders.

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in elementary education built on faith, integrity, and knowledge.

Core Values

  • Faith
  • Respect
  • Discipline
  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Teamwork

School Motto

"Wisdom through faith"
